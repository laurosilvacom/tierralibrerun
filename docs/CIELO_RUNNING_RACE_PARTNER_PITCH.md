## [Brand Name] x Cielo Running

Cielo Running creates thoughtful, community-first trail running experiences.

We are building races that feel well-produced, welcoming, and rooted in the culture of trail running, not just the logistics of race management.

For our next event, we are seeking a small group of partners who want to help bring the race to life and stand behind a strong athlete experience.

## About Cielo Running

Cielo Running is built around a simple belief:

Great races do more than move people through a course. They create memory, belonging, challenge, and connection.

That means we care about:

- a sharp athlete experience
- clear storytelling before race day
- strong on-course support
- thoughtful partner integration
- community credibility, not empty branding

## What partner support makes possible

Partner support directly helps underwrite the parts of a race that athletes feel most:

- permits and insurance
- timing and results
- course marking and safety
- aid stations and athlete fuel
- photography and video
- shirts, bibs, and signage
- volunteer food and logistics
- finish-line experience

This makes the ask tangible.

A partner is not just buying logo placement. A partner is helping create the actual quality of the event.

## Partnership options

### Title or presenting partner

`$15,000-$25,000+`

Best for a brand that wants visible ownership of the event story and a strong on-site presence.

Potential benefits:

- top-tier logo placement
- naming integration where appropriate
- category exclusivity
- branded on-site activation space
- co-branded announcement content
- inclusion in athlete email communications
- post-event recap and reporting

### Supporting partner

`$5,000-$10,000`

Best for a brand that wants meaningful visibility without being the title backer.

Potential benefits:

- logo placement on event materials
- social and email inclusion
- on-site visibility
- product integration where appropriate

### In-kind partner

Best for brands that can contribute product or services instead of cash, including:

- fuel and hydration
- recovery product
- footwear or apparel for prizes
- timing, media, or equipment support

## What a partner receives

Depending on scope, partner value can include:

- access to a highly relevant trail running audience
- credible brand placement within a real event experience
- product trial and sampling opportunities
- digital storytelling before and after race day
- photo and recap assets for partner use
- association with a carefully produced, community-facing event

We are currently looking to confirm:

- 1 financial lead partner
- 1 to 2 supporting partners
- 2 to 4 in-kind partners

If there is alignment, we would love to share a short event overview and discuss a package that makes sense for your goals.
