# Cielo Social Schedule

Use this to keep social useful and contained.

The goal is not to post constantly. The goal is to support registrations, sponsor confidence, and visible momentum.

## Weekly cadence

- 2 feed posts
- 3 story sequences
- 1 collab post every 1 to 2 weeks

## Content rules

Only post within these buckets:

1. why this race exists
2. course or location
3. runner invitation
4. logistics or FAQ
5. partner credibility
6. founder face and voice

## Next 4 weeks

### Week 1

Feed post 1:

- `Cielo Running race announcement`
- Format: single image or simple carousel
- CTA: register or join the interest list

Feed post 2:

- `Rainshadow Running x Cielo Running collab post`
- CTA: shared audience awareness and registration push

Stories:

- founder talking to camera: why this race matters
- course teaser or place-based imagery
- poll: who is coming / what distance are you considering

### Week 2

Feed post 1:

- `Why this race exists`
- Format: text-led carousel
- CTA: this is for runners who want [insert identity of race]

Feed post 2:

- `Alpine Running presence post`
- Keep it simple: one strong image, one paragraph, one CTA

Stories:

- training check-in
- course conditions or logistics note
- repost any runner shares

### Week 3

Feed post 1:

- `Sponsor or partner call`
- Format: clean graphic or founder note
- CTA: brands or local businesses reach out

Feed post 2:

- `Tierra Libre Run community signal`
- Keep it light. This is a presence post, not the main event.

Stories:

- FAQ box
- volunteer call if relevant
- countdown sticker

### Week 4

Feed post 1:

- `Race logistics`
- parking, start time, what to bring, or crew notes

Feed post 2:

- `Last-call registration push`
- urgency without drama

Stories:

- founder reminder
- repost runners registering
- quick sponsor appreciation

## Copy prompts

### Race announcement

`We are building a race that feels sharp, welcoming, and rooted in trail running culture. [Race Name] is open. Come run with us.`

### Why this race exists

`This race exists because the best trail events do more than move people through a course. They create challenge, belonging, and memory.`

### Partner call

`We are currently looking for a small number of race partners to help bring [Race Name] to life. If your brand cares about trail running, athlete experience, and thoughtful community events, let's talk.`

### Last call

`Registration is still open, but not forever. If you've been thinking about it, this is the week to commit.`

## Collab post brief: Rainshadow Running x Cielo Running

Goal:

- borrow trust
- reach a larger trail audience
- convert attention into registrations

What to include:

- shared values or shared trail culture
- race name, date, and location
- why this event matters
- clear CTA to register

Suggested caption start:

`Trail running gets better when more of us build it together. Excited to share [Race Name] with the Rainshadow Running community.`

## Time cap

Do not spend more than:

- 45 minutes writing a feed post
- 20 minutes building story slides
- 30 minutes coordinating a collab post

If it takes longer, simplify it.
